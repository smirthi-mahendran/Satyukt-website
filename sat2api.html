Satyukt API

With our api, users can access satellite data like SM,NDVI,RVI all in a go.

The API is quite simple to use, this page assists you in working with the api.

Satellite Data

Soil Moisture

Soil moisture value determines the amount of water content present in the particular soil type. It can be expressed as a percentage, water by the weight or volume of soil, or inches of water per foot of soil.

NDVI

NDVI stands for Noralized Difference Vegetation Index. It determines the vegetation health condition based on reflectance.Low NDVI indicates stressed vegetation whereas HIgh NDVI indicates healthy vegetation.

RVI

RVI stands for Radar Vegetation Index. It is used for predicting the growth level of crop vegetation over time.Values near zero correspond to bare surfaces while higher values indicate a dense vegetation canopy.

NDVI, EVI, Soil Moisture, xyz tile

The NDVI, EVI, Soil Moisture, xyz tile can be viewed with the following URL:

3.14.103.172:5010/api/info?key=YOUR_KEY&startDate=&endDate=&plotName=

Make sure You enter the start date and the end date.

Note:The dates should be in unix time format plotName must be given with one of the plots you have uploaded

Historical NDVI

You can view the historical NDVI with the URL:

3.14.103.172:5010/api/v1/ndvi?key=YOUR_KEY&startDate=&endDate=&plotName=
