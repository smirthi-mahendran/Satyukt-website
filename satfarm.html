Sat2Farm

My Farm mobile application, a satellite-based agricultural smart phone application, is mainly developed to help the users for understanding their farm health, providing irrigation advisory as well as dealing with weather forecast. It is a simple application wherein the user plots his/her farm boundary and the requirements about the farm are provided through the same.
